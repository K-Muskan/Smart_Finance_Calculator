What is FinAnalyzer?

FinAnalyzer is a smart financial analysis tool that uses numerical methods to solve real-world finance problems such as loan payments, investment growth, and trend analysis.

Why FinAnalyzer?

Most financial calculators only show final answers. FinAnalyzer goes a step further by using numerical techniques to deliver accurate results while helping users understand how those results are achieved.
